Protecting your personal data is important to us. In general, our apps can be used without transmitting personal data to us.

We do not collect, store, or process personal data on our own servers. Data created and used within the apps remains exclusively on your device.

The apps may store the following data locally on your device:

This data:

  • is stored exclusively on your device,
  • is not transmitted to us or to third parties, and

Storage period: Data remains stored until you delete it or uninstall the app.

These permissions are used solely to provide app features, such as capturing photos, recording audio, or selecting and saving images.

No data is transmitted to us or to third parties through the use of these permissions.

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of the app's functions)
